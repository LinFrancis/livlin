Regenerative Inquiry

Multidimensional assessment engine based on the Permaculture Flower.

Challenge it solves: understanding a space's potential across its many dimensions and turning it into an actionable plan.

  • Action plan prioritised by stages
  • Visual progress tracking

Plantae Project · Phytodiversity Explorer

Smart crop database for agroecology and agroforestry.

Challenge it solves: quickly finding what to grow, when and alongside what, without getting lost among scattered sources.

  • Advanced search by characteristics
  • Planting, watering and harvest information
  • Recommended companions and rotations

Theory of Change

Interactive app to build theories of change for impact projects.

Challenge it solves: ordering the logic of an impact project, from the vision to the indicators.

  • Guided definition of vision and goals
  • Mapping of assumptions and indicators
  • Export of a structured document

Participatory Watershed Mapping

Collaborative platform to analyse watershed territories and socio-environmental conflicts.

Challenge it solves: making socio-environmental conflicts in a territory visible and analysing them collaboratively.

  • Exportable directory of actors
